Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 8, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Next Dash DNS ("we", "our", or "us") handles your information when you use our mobile application.

What We Collect

Next Dash DNS does not collect or track any personal or usage data. The only information saved locally is your NextDNS token, which is stored securely:

  • On Android: in Encrypted SharedPreferences
  • On iOS: in the Keychain

Third-Party Services

Next Dash DNS displays information retrieved from NextDNS.io via their public APIs. We do not modify, store, or track any of this data.

Analytics

This app does not use any analytics tools or services. No behavior, usage, or telemetry data is collected.

Data Retention

Your NextDNS token is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server other than NextDNS.io through their official APIs. You can remove the token anytime by removing the token on your device

Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete beyond what is stored locally on your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the app. Updates will be posted on this page with the effective date.
